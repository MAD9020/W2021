# Week 12: Transitions, prototyping, and intro to final project

Learning Objective

By the end of this week, students will:

  • Understand the role of transitions and animations in mobile apps
  • Learn about prototyping methods, tools, and purpose
  • Be introduced to the final project

In-class

  • Lecture
  • Field study – 'Class trip' to initiate final project research
  • Work progress review: Checkout sequence for flower delivery
